Classic Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Crisp edges, chewy middles.
Prep: 15 mins      Cook: 10 mins Additional: 1 hr
Total: 1 hr 25 mins   Servings: 24   Yield: 4 dozen

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup crunchy peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs eggs
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda

Directions

  1. Cream butter, peanut butter, and sugars together in a bowl; beat in eggs.
  2. In a separate bowl, sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt;
  3. Stir into butter mixture. Put dough in refrigerator for 1 hour.
  4. Roll dough into 1 inch balls and put on baking sheets.
  5. Flatten each ball with a fork, making a crisscross pattern.
  6. Bake in a preheated 375 degrees F oven for about 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown.
